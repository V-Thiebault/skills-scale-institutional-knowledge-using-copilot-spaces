# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate Agile ceremonies, remove impediments, and coach the team in Agile practices. They ensure the team follows Scrum principles and continuously improves their processes.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate daily standups, sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives
- Remove blockers and impediments that slow down the team
- Coach team members on Agile and Scrum best practices
- Shield the team from external interruptions and distractions
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement and transparency
- Track team metrics (velocity, burndown) and help optimize workflow

### Goals
- Maximize team productivity and sustainable pace
- Ensure Agile ceremonies are effective and time-boxed
- Cultivate team self-organization and accountability
- Reduce cycle time and improve predictability

### Typical Communication/Collaboration
- Works closely with Project Managers to align on timelines and deliverables
- Collaborates with Product Managers to ensure backlog clarity and refinement
- Coordinates with Developers to identify and remove technical blockers
- Partners with UX Designers and DevOps Engineers to ensure smooth handoffs
- Daily facilitation of standups and regular sprint ceremonies
- Updates on team health, velocity, and impediments

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers create user-centered experiences through research, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing. They ensure that products are intuitive, accessible, and meet user needs.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs
- Collaborate with Product Managers on feature specifications
- Review builds and implementations for usability and design consistency
- Maintain design systems and component libraries
- Advocate for accessibility and inclusive design practices

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, user-friendly experiences
- Reduce user friction and increase satisfaction
- Ensure design consistency across the product
- Validate designs through user feedback and testing

### Typical Communication/Collaboration
- Partners with Product Managers to translate requirements into user flows
- Collaborates with Developers to ensure design feasibility and implementation quality
- Works with Data Analysts to analyze user behavior and inform design decisions
- Coordinates with Project Managers on design timelines and milestones
- Presents designs in sprint reviews and stakeholder meetings
- Provides design specs and assets through handoff documentation

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers manage CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure, and deployment automation. They ensure reliable, secure, and repeatable releases while improving operational efficiency.

### Responsibilities
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines and automation workflows
- Manage infrastructure as code and cloud resources
- Monitor deployments, system health, and performance metrics
- Implement security scanning and compliance checks in pipelines
- Troubleshoot production issues and support incident response
- Collaborate with teams to optimize build and deployment processes

### Goals
- Enable fast, safe, and automated deployments
- Minimize downtime and deployment failures
- Improve observability and monitoring coverage
- Reduce manual operational toil through automation

### Typical Communication/Collaboration
- Works with Developers to optimize build processes and resolve CI/CD issues
- Partners with Project Managers on release planning and deployment windows
- Collaborates with Data Analysts on monitoring and alerting requirements
- Coordinates with Scrum Masters to integrate deployment metrics into team retrospectives
- Provides deployment status updates during standups and release reviews
- Documents runbooks and incident response procedures

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts transform raw data into actionable insights through analysis, reporting, and visualization. They enable data-driven decision-making across product and engineering teams.

### Responsibilities
- Analyze product metrics and user behavior patterns
- Generate reports and dashboards on key performance indicators
- Collaborate with teams to define tracking and analytics requirements
- Validate hypotheses through A/B testing and experiments
- Identify trends, anomalies, and opportunities in data
- Ensure data quality, accuracy, and governance

### Goals
- Enable data-driven product and business decisions
- Provide timely, accurate insights on product performance
- Improve metric visibility and accessibility across teams
- Support experimentation and continuous learning

### Typical Communication/Collaboration
- Partners with Product Managers to define success metrics and analyze outcomes
- Works with Developers to ensure proper instrumentation and event tracking
- Collaborates with UX Designers to analyze user behavior and inform design decisions
- Provides insights to Project Managers on progress toward project goals
- Presents findings in sprint reviews, planning sessions, and stakeholder meetings
- Documents analysis methodologies and metric definitions
